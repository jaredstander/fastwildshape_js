QuickShape, The Pathfinder Wild Shape Calculator

Made for the Paizo Pathfinder Rule Set

This calculator easily generates the new stats of characters using polymorph effects via wild shape, beast shape, elemental body, and plant shape.

UPDATED 8/15/12: Fixed a bug people probably loved involving the calculation of HP when polymorphing. The initial calc gave you tons of extra HP if both the creature you happened to be polymorphing into gave a bonus to CON and your character had a starting CON of 12 or more. This was happening because it was ignoring any bonus HP your starting CON score gave you, essentially giving it to you twice. This has been fixed, and the HP given is now the accurate even when CON is increased or decreased as a result of polymorphing.

UPDATED 7/13/12: With the Advanced Race Guide out, I know that not every player using this calc will have a small or medium-sized creature. Thus, as of Friday, July 13th, all other possible size options have been added for you to choose when indicating your character's size. As such, creatures which are not small or medium require their size be scaled to the closest of these two sizes (indicated in the "Scaled Size" field below) before applying Wild Shape stats.

Please see the following page for reference: Paizo Polymorph Rules.
